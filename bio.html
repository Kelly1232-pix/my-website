Niwenyesiga Kelly Onesmus.

"A Life of Loyalty, Service, and Vision."

Age: 28 years

Born on 29/08/1997 and raised in Uganda, Rukiga District, Butare village. Niwenyesiga Kelly Onesmus is a married to C.K. Kelly with Children and a dedicated, God-fearing young man driven by purpose, service, and a deep sense of family legacy and Charity consciousness. He serves as a police officer under the Special Forces Command (SFC) in Uganda’s Police Presidential Guard(PPG) in those days, a prestigious and trusted role he has carried out with honor since 2019.

In 2022, inspired by his spiritual convictions and strong family values, he founded the The Kellys' Loyal Family — a united, God-centered family devoted to faith, legacy, and impact.

Family and Legacy

Coming from a humble yet purpose-driven background, Kelly is passionate about strengthening families, Prioritizing humanity, and guiding the next generation to walk in faith, wisdom and enthusiasm. His leadership has birthed initiatives such as, the Family Flag, Family Crown, and the ongoing 5 Unified Kellys' Loyal community- based Projects which are community based and under the family name.

  • The Kellys' Loyal Farms.
  • The Kellys' Loyal Foundation.
  • The Kellys' Loyal Medical Centers.
  • The Kellys' Loyal Testimonies.
  • The Kellys' Loyal Schools. ( Bible School and other Schools).

Vision and Future Plans

Kelly’s life mission is rooted in the motto: "God is always our lead!" — a theme that flows through every part of his journey. He envisioned expanding the reach of his family’s legacy through education, outreach, charity-based initiatives with Faith, and development programs across Uganda and beyond.

As he continues to grow in age, wisdom, and service, Kelly seeks to inspire young people to be loyal to God, devoted to purpose, Courageous in Actions and Hopeful in Thoughts..
