Project Overview

The Kellys' Loyal Family is setting up a visionary agricultural project aimed at transforming lives. We have already cultivated an acre of coffee trees in Rwamucucu, Rukiga District, and and we're planning to plant an additional 10,000 trees.

We are expanding into large-scale banana plantations and fish ponds in Mbarara to support food supply for projects like The Kellys' Loyal Foundation which mainly invest its effort to bring hope and smiles to vulnerable people and Orphans. These efforts will also teach communities how agriculture can uplift lives and reduce poverty across Uganda.
